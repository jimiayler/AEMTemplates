Case Study: University Children’s Hospital Basel

The modernisation challenge in a hospital environment
The change of premises at the independent University Children’s Hospital Basel competence centre in 2011 brought about fleet optimisation, standardisation, greater cost transparency, economies in consumables expenditure and happily – simplification.

Exceptional savings and benefits
The advantages of the fleet change were immense. Besides increased system availability and higher data protection, there were reductions in machine-induced noise, waste emission and a decreased burden on the IT and purchasing departments. Most impressively of all, cost savings of 15% to 20% were realised – a financial feat!

CERNER certification

Cerner is a leading supplier of health care information technology solutions that optimise clinical and financial outcomes.
An active partnership exists between Cerner and KYOCERA. We submit specific printers and multifunctionals to Cerner who check whether the devices meet their standards. When all criteria have been fulfilled, a certification is awarded. This offers greater transparency to the end user that the devices are pre-approved for hospital use.
